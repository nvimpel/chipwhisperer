# Starter Kits

NewAE offers several starter packs to make getting into side channel attacks easier. One of these (the ChipSHOUTER)
fall under the ChipSHOUTER branch of products, while the others fall under the ChipWhisperer branch of products.

The following table is a quick comparison between the different ChipWhisperer starter kits.

Note that the ChipWhisperer Pro and its starter kit are both discontinued; it is listed here only for comparison purposes.

| Starter Kit | Capture-Device | Targets | Single Board |  Cost (USD) |
|----------------|--------------|----------|--------------------|-------------------|
| ChipWhisperer-Nano | ChipWhisperer-Nano | STM32F0 | Yes |  $60 |
| ChipWhisperer-Lite | ChipWhisperer-Lite | XMEGA or STM32F3 (CW303) or None | Yes (1-part), No (2-part) |  $368-481 |
| SCAPACK-L1 | Chipwhisperer-Lite | CW308, CW308T-XMEGA, CW308T-STM32F3, CW308T-STM32F0 | No | $850 |
| SCAPACK-L2 | ChipWhisperer-Lite | CW308, CW308T-XMEGA, CW308T-STM32F3, CW308T-STM32F0 | No | $1400 |
| Husky      | ChipWhisperer-Husky      | CW313, CW312T-SAM4S, CW312T-iCE40             | No | $643 |
| Husky-Plus | ChipWhisperer-Husky-Plus | CW313, CW312T-SAM4S, CW312T-iCE40             | No | $1115 |
| <s>ChipWhisperer-Pro</s> | <s>ChipWhisperer-Pro</s> | <s>CW308, CW308T-XMEGA, CW308T-STM32F3</s> | No | *discontinued* |

| SCAPACK-L1 | micro-usb cable, CW308T Generic PCB, CW308T-STM32F blank PCB, 2x Lifter tool to remove targets, SMA Tee adapter, SMA Cables, SMA to BNC Cable | 
| SCAPACK-L2 | micro-usb cable, CW308T Generic PCB, CW308T-STM32F blank PCB, 2x Lifter tool to remove targets, SMA Tee adapter, SMA Cables, SMA to BNC Cable, H-Field Probe, Differential Probe, Probe power supply (DC-DC), 5V wall adapter for probe/CW308 power | 
| Husky      | SMA Cables (×2), ChipWhisperer 20-Pin Connector breakout wires, MCX to SMA & MCX to BNC, 20-Pin Cables (×2), USB-C Cable (with USB-A adapter), Jumper Wires & Caps, CW308 Target to CW312 adapter |
| Husky-Plus | SMA Cables (×2), ChipWhisperer 20-Pin Connector breakout wires, MCX to SMA & MCX to BNC, 20-Pin Cables (×2), USB-C Cable (with USB-A adapter), Jumper Wires & Caps, CW308 Target to CW312 adapter |
| <s>ChipWhisperer-Pro</s> *(discontinued)* | <s>USB A to B cable, CW506 advanced breakout (level shifter), H-Field Probe, Low Noise Amplifier, Differential Probe, Probe Power Supply (Isolated), 20MHz low-pass filter, SMA to BNC cable, SMA to SMA cable, CW308T Generic PCB, CW308T-STM32F Blank PCB, SMA adapters, 5V power supply</s> |


The ChipSHOUTER starter kit comes with the ChipSHOUTER, as well as 2 EMFI targets and a bunch of 
cabling/converters for attaching to different lab equipment.
